Data Sharks

Combine strategy and cyberwarfare and this is what you get. A full-scale browser based game built on HTML5 Canvas and Javascript that teaches you how important cyber infrastructure can be in the modern age. Attack your rivaling countries, and see how your equipment holds against theirs. But if you don't win, they will fight back to steal your info, with more hackers and better servers!
