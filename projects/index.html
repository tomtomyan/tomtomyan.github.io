Revolve

Revolve isn't a simple platformer like Super Mario Bros., it challenges the player to think about every movement. Effective gravity manipulation is key to nagivate the diverse range of blocks and mechanics. It demands endless replayability, as you will be constantly trying to find a new path to victory, or outrun your own high score. Created using Java Graphics2D library and Eclipse.

Data Sharks

Combine strategy and cyberwarfare and this is what you get. A full-scale browser based game built on HTML5 Canvas and Javascript that teaches you how important cyber infrastructure can be in the modern age. Attack your rivaling countries, and see how your equipment holds against theirs. But if you don't win, they will fight back to steal your info, with more hackers and better servers!
